Playing your Quotes in the Popup QuotePlayer

In order to open the QuotePlayer, click on the icon in the row of installed extensions of Chrome.

Inside the QuotePlayer you have a few options to chose from:

  • set the interval in which a new Quote shall be shown
  • select a playlist in the drop-down, that you want to play
  • start play_arrow or stop stop the QuotePlayer
  • open the Admin Page (click on ), where you can manage your Quotes and also manage Playlists and report a bug
