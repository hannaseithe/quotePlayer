Playing your Quotes in the QuotePlayer

In order to open the QuotePlayer, click on the extension icon in Chrome Toolbar

Inside the QuotePlayer you have a few options to chose from:

set the  interval  in which a new Quote shall be shown

select a playlist in the drop-down, that you want to play

start play_arrow or stop stop the QuotePlayer.
warningWhile the QuotePlayer is playing, the extension icon in the toolbar is orange

open the Admin Page (click on  Manage Quotes), where you can manage your Quotes and also manage Playlists and report a bug
