The Quote notifications

Depending on your Operating System, your Chrome Version and Chrome Settings, different kinds of Notifications will be used to play your Quotes to you.

Chrome Notifications

Chrome Notifications look like this

the Next Quote button close the notification and play the next Quote after the Interval, that you set in the QuotePlayer.

the Stop button, will close the notification and stop the Player. No next Quote will be played.y

Windows Notifications

Windows Notifications look like this

the Next Quote button close the notification and play the next Quote after the Interval, that you set in the QuotePlayer.

the Stop button, will close the notification and stop the Player. No next Quote will be played.y

this Hide in action center button will Hide the notification in the windows action center.
warning The notification is still open (but hidden)! In order to stop or play the next quote, you will have to open the Windows action center and close or next-quote the notification there.
