Add Quotes to your Playlist

In this Dialog you can add Quotes from your Quotes-Database to your currently selected Playlist

Click on the select field to select all shown quotes

Click on the select field of a single quote to select that one quote

Quotes that have already been added to your playlist will be greyed in. You are free to add them multiple times though.

Once you have selected all Quotes that you want to add to your playlist, click Add selected Quote(s). The quotes will be added to your playlist.
