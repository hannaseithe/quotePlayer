How to manage a single playlist

There are three things you can do here:

  1. Delete a Quote (the Quote will be only deleted from the playlist, it will still be available in the Quotes Database
  2. Reorder the Quotes: Hover with your mouse over a quote, you want to reorder, then press the left mousebutton. Then while holding the mousebutton pressed move the quote to a different position in the list. And let go of the mousebutton. The Quotes will be reordered automatically
  3. Add new Quote(s): Click on the Button . This will open a dialog, with a list of all quotes in your database. Select one or more quotes to be added to your playlist. And then click the button . The selected Quotes will be automatically added.
