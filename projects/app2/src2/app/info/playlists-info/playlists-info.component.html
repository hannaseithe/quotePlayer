Managing your Playlists

Here you can create, edit and delete your playlists.

In order to play your quotes in the QuotePlayer Popup, you will first have to add them to a playlist, which you then will select in the QuotePlayer Popup.

To add a new Playlist click on the button "Add a new Playlist in the blue navigation bar, fill out the input field and press Enter. The new (and empty) playlist will be automatically added.

In the Left Table you can select a Playlist that you want to edit, by clicking on it. Furthermore you can delete delete the Playlist or editedit the name of the Playlist

Once you have selected a Playlist on the Left, the Right Table will show the selected Playlist with its Quotes. Here you can:

Add Quotes that are already saved in your Database

deletedelete Quotes from your Playlist

change the order of the quotes (as they will be played in the QuotePlayer Popup).
Hover with your mouse over a quote, you want to reorder, then press the left mousebutton. Then while holding the mousebutton pressed move the quote to a different position in the list. And let go of the mousebutton. The Quotes will be reordered automatically

warningChanges to a Playlist that is currently playing in the QuotePlayer will only show after you stop and start the QuotePlayer again
